🚀 Never Miss Your Stop Again — Meet the Distance Alarm App!
Turn your phone into a smart travel companion with our Distance Alarm App, built with React Native and powered by OpenStreetMap 🌍
📍 How it works:
Tap anywhere on the map or search for a place to set your destination
Choose your alert distance (100m, 500m, 1km & more)
Sit back and relax while the app tracks your journey in real time
🔔 Smart Alerts, Right on Time
Get notified with sound, vibration, or voice alert exactly when you reach your chosen distance — even if the app is running in the background.
✨ Powerful Features:
• Real-time distance tracking with high GPS accuracy
• Background tracking (works even when your screen is off)
• Custom radius alerts with easy slider control
• Multiple destination support (set more than one stop)
• Save favorite places for quick access ⭐
• Smart notifications with location name & distance
• Battery-optimized tracking for long trips 🔋
• Offline map support using OpenStreetMap
• Route preview and live path tracking 🗺️
• Dark mode for comfortable night use 🌙
